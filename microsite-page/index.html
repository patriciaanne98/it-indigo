Meet Patricia

Hi, I’m Patricia — a certified Pilates instructor passionate about helping people move better, feel stronger, and build confidence in their bodies.

My teaching style blends strong fundamentals with a modern, approachable feel. Whether you’re completely new to Pilates or looking to deepen your practice, each session is designed to meet you where you are.

From mat work to reformer sessions, my goal is simple: help you leave class feeling supported, challenged, and proud of yourself.
