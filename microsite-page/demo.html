What to notice

  • Randomness feels exciting. Even “no win” can keep you going.
  • The next pull might be the one. That belief is the hook.
  • Apps simulate pulls via refresh, notifications, “suggested posts,” autoplay.
Connect the dots: When your feed is ranked by AI, it can learn which content keeps you engaged longer, then keep serving more of it.
